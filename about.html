Meet Jacy Mozingo

Faith, family, service, and a heart for every animal.

Falling Feathers Hollow is the vision of Jacy Mozingo, a U.S. Army veteran who served in Afghanistan, mother of four, Christian, and lifelong animal lover.

Founder • Veteran • Mother • Christian

A life shaped by service and compassion.

Jacy Mozingo brings the same strength, responsibility, and perseverance she carried through military service into the everyday work of caring for her family, her community, and the animals of Falling Feathers Hollow.

Her Christian faith is at the center of how she lives and serves. She believes care should be practical, consistent, and rooted in love, whether that means helping a neighbor, volunteering, raising four children, or giving an animal a safe place to land.

Family, faith, and service

Strong roots make a strong Hollow.

Falling Feathers Hollow is supported by a family that understands teamwork, resilience, and showing up for one another. The work may begin with feeding, cleaning, and collecting eggs, but its deeper purpose is teaching responsibility, kindness, and care.

🐾
A lifelong love for animals

Every animal deserves thoughtful care.

What started with a love for ducks has grown into a caring home for chickens, quail, and animals that sometimes need a little extra attention. The goal is to remain personal, responsible, clean, and centered on the animals.
