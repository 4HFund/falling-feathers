Our Story

Falling Feathers Hollow is a sanctuary-style backyard farm focused on responsible animal care, local eggs, and building a peaceful place where purpose grows through everyday stewardship.

About Falling Feathers Hollow

Founded by Jacy, Falling Feathers Hollow is a woman veteran-owned project built around animal care, practical stewardship, and a deep respect for the flock.

What began with ducks has grown into a small but meaningful sanctuary-style farm with Pekins, Rouens, Runner ducklings, chickens, quail, and rescue animals. Each part of the hollow is being developed with care, patience, and a focus on the animals’ well-being.

The goal is simple: provide safe, clean, thoughtful care for the animals while offering local Chicken, Duck, & Quail Eggs when supply allows. The operation is intentionally small, seasonal, and personal, with the animals always coming first.

Falling Feathers Hollow is also a place of learning and community. It reflects the values of responsibility, resilience, family support, and the belief that meaningful work can begin right at home.
