About Falling Feathers Hollow

Founded by Jacy, Falling Feathers Hollow is built around animal care, practical stewardship, and a deep respect for the flock.

What began with ducks has grown into a small but meaningful sanctuary-style farm with ducks, ducklings, chickens, quail, and rescue animals. The flock changes as animals grow and new needs arise, so the focus is not on fixed numbers. The focus is on care.

The goal is simple: provide safe, clean, thoughtful care for the animals while offering local Chicken, Duck, & Quail Eggs when supply allows. The operation is intentionally small, seasonal, and hands-on, with the animals always coming first.

Falling Feathers Hollow also reflects values of responsibility, resilience, family support, and community connection. It is a place where meaningful work starts at home and grows one improvement at a time.
