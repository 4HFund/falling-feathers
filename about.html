Jacy’s Hollow

Falling Feathers Hollow is Jacy’s project, her passion, and her way of turning love for ducks, chickens, quail, and rescue animals into something meaningful for the whole community.

About Jacy and the Heart Behind It

Falling Feathers Hollow is led by Jacy, a woman veteran with a deep love for animals and a heart for building something gentle, useful, and full of purpose.

This is her vision. Her hands are in the daily care, the planning, the learning, and the dreaming. The ducks, chickens, quail, and rescues are not just animals on a page. They are the reason this project exists.

As a woman veteran-owned project, Falling Feathers Hollow is about more than selling eggs. It is about stewardship, patience, healing, and creating a peaceful place where animals are cared for well and people can see the value in small, faithful work.

The family supports the dream, but the heart of the hollow is Jacy’s passion for the flock. Every coop improvement, every rescued bird, every egg carton, and every step forward points back to her love for these animals.
