Meet the FlockOur Story

Built with care and purpose

Falling Feathers Hollow is a small, hands-on sanctuary project rooted in animal care, practical stewardship, and community connection.

The operation is intentionally small and seasonal. The animals come first, and every part of the hollow is being developed with safety, cleanliness, and long-term care in mind.

The Current Flock

The hollow is growing quickly, with Pekins, Rouens, Runner ducklings, quail, chickens, and a rooster all part of the daily care routine.
