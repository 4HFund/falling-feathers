Our mission

A premium small-farm experience with a sanctuary heart.

We are building a clean, intentional, family-supported hollow where animals come first. The flock may change as animals grow and new rescues arrive, but the standard stays the same: safe spaces, careful routines, and responsible stewardship.

When the flock is laying and supply allows, we offer Chicken, Duck, & Quail Eggs with the same care and honesty that guides the rest of the hollow.
