Meet the flock

Every feather has a place.

The flock at Falling Feathers Hollow grows and changes over time, so this page highlights the groups we care for rather than keeping a running inventory. The focus stays on safe housing, clean routines, and thoughtful stewardship.

Our standard

Small-scale care with a professional standard.

The goal is not to look like a big commercial farm. The goal is to look intentional, clean, trustworthy, and rooted in genuine care. The flock may grow, shift, and change, but the promise stays the same.
