Meet Our Flock

Chicken, Duck, & Quail Eggs

When the flock is laying and supply allows, Falling Feathers Hollow offers local eggs produced through small-scale, hands-on care.

Quail Eggs

For Families and Four-Legged Friends

Some customers ask about eggs for their dogs, especially quail eggs. We keep that information simple and honest.

Quail Eggs for Dogs

Many pet owners use quail eggs as an occasional whole-food topper or special treat. Start small, keep it balanced, and check with your vet if your dog has allergies or health concerns.

Local and Seasonal

Egg availability changes with the flock, the weather, and the season. We would rather be honest about supply than overpromise.

Care Comes First

This is not a factory setup. The animals come first, and the eggs are part of a larger mission of stewardship, care, and community.

Built feather by feather

Falling Feathers Hollow is a woman veteran-owned sanctuary-style project where animals are cared for carefully, families can learn, and small-scale farming is handled with purpose.
